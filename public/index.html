QUIS?

QUIS match you with a native speaker of the language you want to practice.
Then one after the other ask questions to guess which animal you are!
Try to speak as much as possible in the foreign language, avoid English. Correct and help your partner, you are both here to learn!
Have fun!

This wbpage is a simple version of the application Quis, a project developped for my BA thesis.
In order to help me improve this project, please share with me your experience on QUIS
